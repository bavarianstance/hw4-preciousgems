Precious Gems Game

You'll be given a random number at the start of the game. This is your target score. There are eight precious gems below. Click on a gem and you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You'll win if your total score equals the target score. Go over, and you lose. You'll only find out the value of each precious gem by your powers of deduction. Each time you start a new game - the gem values will reset. Good luck!
